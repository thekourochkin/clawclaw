Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

How is PrivatClaw different from SimpleClaw?

While SimpleClaw offers fast deployment on shared infrastructure, PrivatClaw gives you your own isolated server with end-to-end encryption. We also support 10+ channels (vs just Telegram), custom AI models, and enterprise features like SSO, audit logs, and HIPAA compliance. Same speed, infinitely more secure.

Do I really need technical skills to deploy?

Absolutely not. No SSH keys, no terminal commands, no server configuration. Just sign in, pick your AI model, connect your channel (Telegram, Slack, etc.), and click deploy. The entire process takes less than 60 seconds.

Is my data really private?

Yes. Every PrivatClaw instance runs on your own isolated server. We use end-to-end encryption, zero-knowledge architecture, and never log your conversations. Your data never touches our servers and never trains AI models. We literally can't access it even if we wanted to.

Which AI models can I use?

We support OpenAI (GPT-3.5, GPT-4), Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and local models like Llama. You can switch between models anytime without redeployment. Enterprise customers can even run their own fine-tuned models on-premise.

What channels are supported?

Currently: Telegram, WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, Email, SMS, Microsoft Teams, Messenger, WeChat, and LINE. We're constantly adding more based on user requests. The same AI assistant works across all channels simultaneously.

Can I try before committing?

Absolutely! Our Starter plan is completely free forever (1,000 messages/month, 2 channels). For Professional features, we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. If you're not satisfied, we also have a 14-day money-back guarantee.

Is this GDPR/HIPAA compliant?

Yes. We're GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 Type II compliant. Our isolated infrastructure, encryption standards, and audit logging meet the strictest compliance requirements. We provide BAAs for healthcare customers and DPAs for GDPR compliance.

What if I need help or have issues?

Free users get community support via Discord. Professional users get priority email support (response within 4 hours). Enterprise customers get 24/7 dedicated support with a guaranteed SLA and a dedicated account manager.
